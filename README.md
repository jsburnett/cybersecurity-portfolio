
## About Me
I'm a Marine veteran turned IT leader based in Alabama, currently serving as CTO at a local rural electric cooperative. When I'm not knee-deep in infrastructure, I'm probably watching one of my three kids compete in something, gaming, or listening to a new Audible book. I have been married to my best friend for almost 20 years and can't imagine life without my family. 
I was brought up doing HVAC and electrical work before I found my way into IT. No CS degree to lean on, just a lot of broken things that needed fixing and a stubborn refusal to leave them that way. That path eventually landed me managing enterprise infrastructure for a critical utility: multi-domain Active Directory, VMware, the Fortinet security stack, SCADA/OT networks, and the kind of systems where downtime is a lot more than an inconvenience. I've done everything from DC promotions at 2am to incident response on production, and somewhere along the way I learned that the best documentation is the kind you write before something breaks.
These days my focus has shifted hard toward cybersecurity, and I keep pushing deeper into it. I hold several GIAC certifications including GSEC, GCIH, and GCIA, and I'm finishing my Bachelor's in Applied Cybersecurity at SANS Technology Institute. I'm hoping this portfolio works as a journal of sorts for that journey: a place to track what I'm learning, document the tools I build, and collect the resources worth keeping.
